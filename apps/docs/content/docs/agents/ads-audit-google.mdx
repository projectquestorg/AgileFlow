# Ads Audit Google

Google Ads auditor that analyzes Google Ads account data and scores it across 74 deterministic checks in 6 weighted categories to identify optimization opportunities and compliance issues.

## When to Use

Use this agent when:

- Running comprehensive Google Ads account audit
- Identifying conversion tracking issues
- Analyzing wasted spend and optimization opportunities
- Reviewing account structure and organization
- Auditing keyword strategy and quality scores
- Checking ad copy quality and performance
- Validating campaign settings and configurations

## How It Works

1. **Accepts Account Data**: Export CSV, screenshots, or structured text descriptions
2. **Applies 74 Checks**: Across 6 weighted categories
3. **Enforces Quality Gates**: Conversion tracking required, no Broad Match without Smart Bidding, 3x Kill Rule
4. **Identifies Issues**: Tags each finding with severity (CRITICAL, HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW)
5. **Scores Overall Health**: Outputs Google Ads Score out of 100

## Focus Areas

- **Conversion Tracking (25%)** - 12 checks on tag installation, conversion actions, attribution
- **Wasted Spend (25%)** - 15 checks on negative keywords, search terms, quality scores
- **Account Structure (15%)** - 12 checks on naming conventions, ad groups, campaign organization
- **Keyword Strategy (15%)** - 14 checks on match types, quality scores, intent alignment
- **Ad Copy Quality (10%)** - 11 checks on RSAs, headlines, CTAs, extensions
- **Campaign Settings (10%)** - 10 checks on bidding, location targeting, remarketing

## Tools Available

This agent has access to: Read, Glob, Grep

## Example Usage

```text
Task(
  description: "Comprehensive Google Ads audit",
  prompt: "Google Ads account: 8 campaigns, 40 ad groups, 500+ keywords. Monthly spend $15K. Using mostly Broad Match keywords with manual bids. CTR 2%, CPC $3, CPA $50 target.",
  subagent_type: "ads-audit-google"
)
```

## Key Capabilities

- **Conversion tracking validation** - Tag, enhanced conversions, consent mode
- **Wasted spend identification** - Negative keywords, search terms, low QS
- **Account structure assessment** - Naming, ad group organization, PMax isolation
- **Keyword strategy evaluation** - Match types, quality score distribution, intent alignment
- **Ad copy quality review** - RSA count, headline quality, extension usage
- **Campaign settings validation** - Bidding strategy, location targeting, audience exclusions

## Quality Gates

1. **Conversion tracking required** - No optimization recommendations without tracking
2. **No Broad Match without Smart Bidding** - Critical gate prevents wasted spend
3. **3x Kill Rule** - Campaigns with CPA > 3x target must be flagged CRITICAL
4. **Brand/non-brand separation** - Must be separate for accurate measurement
