# Ads Audit Budget

Budget auditor that analyzes ad spend allocation and bidding strategies across platforms, applying 24 deterministic checks for spend efficiency, scaling rules, and industry benchmarks.

## When to Use

Use this agent when:

- Optimizing budget allocation across platforms
- Setting up or reviewing bidding strategies
- Analyzing budget scaling and pacing rules
- Checking platform spend minimums
- Benchmarking against industry standards
- Managing campaign budget distribution

## How It Works

1. **Validates Account Data**: Accepts spend data, campaign budgets, bidding strategies
2. **Applies 24 Checks**: Across 4 categories (Budget Allocation, Bidding Strategy, Scaling & Pacing, Platform Mix)
3. **Enforces Rules**: Platform minimums, scaling caps, 3x Kill Rule
4. **Generates Recommendations**: Specific dollar reallocation suggestions
5. **Scores Budget Health**: Outputs Budget Score out of 100

## Focus Areas

- **Budget Allocation (35%)** - 8 checks on spend distribution and top-performer focus
- **Bidding Strategy (30%)** - 8 checks on strategy alignment and conversion data requirements
- **Scaling & Pacing (20%)** - 4 checks on budget growth rates and learning phase compliance
- **Platform Mix (15%)** - 4 checks on platform diversification and attribution

## Tools Available

This agent has access to: Read, Glob, Grep

## Example Usage

```text
Task(
  description: "Analyze our budget allocation across Google and Meta",
  prompt: "We're spending $10K/month total. Google gets $6K (4 campaigns), Meta gets $4K (6 ad sets). Need to reallocate based on ROAS.",
  subagent_type: "ads-audit-budget"
)
```

## Key Capabilities

- **Budget scoring** with weighted category analysis
- **Platform minimum enforcement** across 6 platforms
- **Scaling rule validation** (max 20% per week)
- **Industry benchmark comparison** for SaaS, E-commerce, Local Services, B2B
- **Specific reallocation recommendations** with dollar amounts
- **Bid strategy alignment** with campaign objectives

## Platform Budget Minimums

| Platform | Campaign Minimum | Ad Set/Group Minimum |
|----------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Google Ads | $10/day | $5/day |
| Meta Ads | $20/day | $10/day |
| LinkedIn | $50/day | $25/day |
| TikTok | $50/day | $20/day |
| Microsoft | $10/day | $5/day |

## Quality Gates

- **Platform minimums are non-negotiable** - Below minimums = CRITICAL finding
- **Conversion data required** - Must have 30+ conversions/month for Smart Bidding recommendations
- **Learning phase protection** - No changes during learning windows
- **3x Kill Rule** - Flag campaigns with CPA > 3x target for immediate pause
