# Ads Audit Compliance

Compliance auditor that analyzes advertising accounts for policy compliance, regulatory requirements, and performance against industry benchmarks, applying 18 deterministic checks.

## When to Use

Use this agent when:

- Auditing account for policy violations or disapprovals
- Ensuring regulatory compliance (GDPR, CCPA, COPPA)
- Checking performance against industry benchmarks
- Verifying special ad category declarations (housing, employment, credit)
- Assessing account health and payment status
- Identifying legal risk in ad campaigns

## How It Works

1. **Parses Account Data**: Campaign data, ad copy, targeting, regulatory context
2. **Applies 18 Checks**: Across 4 categories (Platform Policy, Regulatory, Benchmarks, Account Health)
3. **Identifies Legal Risk**: Flags findings that carry legal liability
4. **Benchmarks Performance**: Compares against industry standards
5. **Generates Compliance Report**: With severity levels and remediation steps

## Focus Areas

- **Platform Policy Compliance (35%)** - 6 checks on ad disapprovals, trademarks, landing pages
- **Regulatory Compliance (30%)** - 5 checks on GDPR, CCPA, FTC, COPPA requirements
- **Performance Benchmarks (20%)** - 4 checks on CTR, CPA, CVR vs industry standards
- **Account Health (15%)** - 3 checks on quality score, payment status, access controls

## Tools Available

This agent has access to: Read, Glob, Grep

## Example Usage

```text
Task(
  description: "Compliance audit for housing ads campaign",
  prompt: "We're running ads for rental properties across Google and Meta. Need to verify housing policy compliance and GDPR consent implementation.",
  subagent_type: "ads-audit-compliance"
)
```

## Key Capabilities

- **Policy compliance checking** across 6 major ad platforms
- **Special Ad Category verification** (housing, employment, credit, political)
- **Regulatory requirement validation** (GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, FTC)
- **Industry benchmark comparison** across 8 verticals
- **Legal risk identification** and severity flagging
- **Remediation recommendations** with timelines

## Special Ad Categories

| Category | Google | Meta | LinkedIn | TikTok |
|----------|--------|------|----------|--------|
| Housing | Required | Required | N/A | Limited |
| Employment | Required | Required | Built-in | Limited |
| Credit/Financial | Required | Required | N/A | Limited |
| Political | Required | Required | Prohibited | Prohibited |
| Alcohol | Restricted | Restricted | Restricted | 21+ targeting |
| Pharmaceuticals | Certification | Restricted | Restricted | Prohibited |
| Gambling | Certification | Certification | Prohibited | Prohibited |

## Quality Gates

- **Ad disapprovals** are emergencies - Fix immediately to restore serving
- **Special categories** are legal requirements - Non-compliance = suspension risk
- **GDPR/CCPA violations** are legal liability - Failure = financial penalties
- **COPPA violations** are most serious - Up to $50K per violation
